Khajuraho

Famous for its stunning group of Hindu and Jain temples, Khajuraho is recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage site. These temples, built between 950 and 1050 AD, are renowned for their intricate carvings, especially those depicting erotic and sensuous themes. Khajuraho offers a unique architectural brilliance and spiritual ambiance, perfect for wedding shoots.

Discover temples, palaces, and memorials within this majestic complex that takes you back to the grandeur of India's rich history.

Location Features:

: Chhatarpur District, Madhya Pradesh

Raneh waterfalls

Raneh Waterfalls are created by the Ken River and cascade through a deep canyon made of volcanic granite. The multicolored rock formations create an ethereal background, making it ideal for pre-wedding photoshoots. The surrounding forested area adds a natural charm to the site.

Location Features:

: Around 20 km from Khajuraho, Chhatarpur District

Kalinjar Fort

Kalinjar Fort is an ancient hill fort dating back to the 10th century. Known for its historical significance, it offers an adventurous and rustic background. With panoramic views, it provides excellent backdrops for pre-wedding shoots, particularly for those looking for a royal or historical theme.

Location Features:

: Near Banda, 80 km from Khajuraho,Chhatarpur District

Gulganj Fort

A lesser-known but picturesque fort in the region, perfect for wedding shoots that seek a more offbeat and untouched venue. Its architecture and location offer a quiet, secluded atmosphere for intimate celebrations and photography.

Location Features:

: Gulganj, Chhatarpur District

Bhagwan Parshwanath Digambar Jain Temple

This Jain temple is part of the eastern group of temples in Khajuraho. The tranquil and pristine environment here adds a spiritual tone to wedding shoots, with intricate carvings providing a delicate backdrop for photography.

Location Features:

: Khajuraho, Chhatarpur District

Kandariya Mahadev Temple

One of the largest and most ornate temples in Khajuraho, dedicated to Lord Shiva. The towering spire and detailed carvings make this temple one of the most sought-after locations for both weddings and pre-wedding shoots..

Location Features:

: Khajuraho, Chhatarpur District

Chaturbhuj Temple

This temple is known for its towering structure and a large idol of Vishnu. Its quiet surroundings make it ideal for a peaceful and spiritual photoshoot, providing a tranquil escape from more crowded locations..

Location Features:

: Khajuraho, Chhatarpur District

Lakshmana Temple

Dedicated to Vishnu, this temple stands out for its artistic and architectural excellence. The intricate sculptures and grand scale of the temple make it an ideal location for elaborate wedding ceremonies and stunning photoshoots..

Location Features:

: Khajuraho, Chhatarpur District
