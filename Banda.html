Mahaeshwari Devi Temple

This temple is dedicated to Goddess Maheshwari, a manifestation of Goddess Durga. It's a revered religious site, often visited by devotees seeking blessings. The temple has a serene ambiance with traditional architecture.

Discover temples, palaces, and memorials within this majestic complex that takes you back to the grandeur of India's rich history.

Location Features:

Banda, Madhya Pradesh

Nawab Tank

Nawab Tank is a scenic water body located amidst nature in Banda. It provides a peaceful environment and picturesque views with lush green surroundings.

Location Features:

Banda, Madhya Pradesh

Kalinjar Fort

Kalinjar Fort is a historical fort dating back to the 10th century. It has a rich history and is known for its strategic importance in medieval India. The fort offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

Location Features:

Banda, Madhya Pradesh

Bhuragarh Fort

Bhuragarh Fort is another historical fort located near the Ken River in Banda. The fort is less known but holds an enchanting charm with its ruins and scenic location.

Location Features:

Banda, Madhya Pradesh

Bamdev Temple

Bamdev Temple is dedicated to Rishi Bamdev and is an ancient site of religious significance. It is surrounded by nature and holds a peaceful and tranquil vibe.

Location Features:

Banda, Madhya Pradesh
