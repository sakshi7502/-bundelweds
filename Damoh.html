Jatashankar Mandir

This temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is surrounded by natural caves, giving it a serene and spiritual ambiance. The temple is nestled among hills, making it a peaceful pilgrimage spot..

Discover temples, palaces, and memorials within this majestic complex that takes you back to the grandeur of India's rich history.

Location Features:

: Located in the Patharia region of Damoh district

Queen Damayanti Museum and Fort

This historic fort and museum are associated with Queen Damayanti, a legendary figure in Indian mythology. The site includes a small museum showcasing local artifacts and a fort that holds historical importance in the region.

Location Features:

: Situated within Damoh town.

Balatal Pond

Balatal Pond is a large and scenic water body surrounded by greenery, offering a serene environment perfect for leisure and photography.

Location Features:

: Situated within Damoh town

Hirdepur Lake

This is a large and scenic lake located amidst lush greenery, offering peaceful surroundings and panoramic views.

Location Features:

: Around 25 km from Damoh town.

Clock Tower Circuit

The Clock Tower is a historical monument and a well-known landmark in Damoh. It is located at the center of town and serves as a hub for local activities.

Location Features:

: Situated in the heart of Damoh town.

Nadan Fall

Nadan Fall is a beautiful waterfall located in the midst of a forest area, offering a refreshing and scenic landscape. It is a popular local tourist spot, especially during the monsoon season.

Location Features:

: Around 35 km from Damoh town, near Jabera.

Bhadbhada Ghat

Bhadbhada Ghat is a scenic area with waterfalls and rocky outcrops, creating a serene environment, especially after the monsoon season when the waterfalls are in full flow.

Location Features:

: Situated near Damoh town.
