Gadpehra Temple (Garhpahra Temple)

Located about 10 km from Sagar, Gadpehra Temple (also known as Garhpahra) is dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple is perched on a hill, offering a panoramic view of the surrounding landscape. The scenic beauty and ancient architecture make it an ideal spot for pre-wedding shoots with a religious or traditional theme.

Location Features:

: Near Sagar, Madhya Pradesh

Eran

Known for its historical and archaeological significance, Eran is famous for ancient temples, inscriptions, and sculptures, dating back to the Gupta period (4th to 6th centuries AD). The blend of history, culture, and rustic charm at Eran makes it a fascinating backdrop for pre-wedding photoshoots, especially for those looking to incorporate ancient heritage into their themes.

Location Features:

: Around 75 km from Sagar, Madhya Pradesh

Rahatgarh Waterfall

Rahatgarh Waterfall is a beautiful natural site formed by the Bina River. The waterfall cascades down a series of rocks and is surrounded by lush greenery, creating a stunning background for both wedding and pre-wedding shoots. The sound of the waterfall and the natural beauty make this location ideal for couples who want an outdoor, nature-inspired theme.

Location Features:

: Around 40 km from Sagar, Madhya Pradesh

Lakha Banjara Lake(Sagar Lake)

Sagar Lake, also known as Lakha Banjara Lake, is a popular destination within the city of Sagar. Surrounded by temples and hills, the lake provides a calm, serene environment, ideal for romantic and intimate wedding or pre-wedding shoots. The beautiful sunsets, reflections on the water, and nearby greenery offer ample opportunities for scenic photography.

Location Features:

: Sagar, Madhya Pradesh
