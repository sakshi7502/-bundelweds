Kundeshwar

Kundeshwar is a famous temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, situated on the banks of the Jamdhar River. The temple complex is serene, surrounded by natural beauty and a holy river, making it a popular pilgrimage spot.

Location Features:

: 5 km from Tikamgarh, Madhya Pradesh.

Baladevgarh

A town known for its ancient temples, particularly the Baladeoji Temple, which has historical and architectural significance. The area is rich in heritage and history, with peaceful surroundings.

Location Features:

: Near Tikamgarh, Madhya Pradesh.

Aharji (Aharji Jain Temple)

Aharji is a Jain pilgrimage center, known for its peaceful environment and stunning temple architecture. The site is dedicated to Lord Adinath and holds religious importance.

Location Features:

: Near Tikamgarh, Madhya Pradesh.

Papora Ji (Jain Temple)

APapora Ji is a Jain pilgrimage site that attracts devotees due to its peaceful atmosphere and beautiful temples. The place is associated with ancient Jain traditions and is well-maintained.

Location Features:

: Near Tikamgarh, Madhya Pradesh.

Umri

Umri is known for its natural beauty and ancient heritage. It has religious significance and a serene, rural charm.

Location Features:

: Near Tikamgarh, Madhya Pradesh

Orchha Fort

Orchha Fort is a magnificent historical fort built by the Bundela rulers in the 16th century. The fort complex includes palaces, temples, and gardens, offering breathtaking views and a regal atmosphere.

Location Features:

: Orchha, Tikamgarh, Madhya Pradesh.

Chaturbhuj Temple

The Ram Raja Temple is a unique temple where Lord Rama is worshipped as a king. The temple is both a spiritual and architectural marvel.

Location Features:

: Orchha, Tikamgarh, Madhya Pradesh

Jahangir Mahal

Jahangir Mahal is a palace in Orchha built by Raja Bir Singh Deo to honor Emperor Jahangir. It boasts grand Mughal architecture and royal charm.

Location Features:

: Orchha, Tikamgarh, Madhya Pradesh.

Raj Mahal

The Raj Mahal is another palace in Orchha, known for its grandeur and intricate frescoes. It was once the residence of the Bundela kings and is steeped in history.

Location Features:

: Orchha, Tikamgarh, Madhya Pradesh.

Chhatris (Cenotaphs)

The Chhatris of Orchha are cenotaphs built to honor the Bundela rulers. These grand structures are located by the Betwa River and are known for their beautiful architectural design.

Location Features:

: Orchha, Tikamgarh, Madhya Pradesh.
